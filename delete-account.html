Delete Your Account & Data

Creaso-Norse Technologies LLC
Last updated: July 2, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated personal data from applications published by Creaso-Norse Technologies LLC. It applies to our applications distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store that offer account creation or data storage.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

If the application you use offers accounts, the fastest way to delete your account and data is directly in the app:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings (or your Profile / Account screen).
  2. Select Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the request. Your account and associated data will be deleted.

If an app does not include a Delete Account option, use the email request below.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by email. Click the button below (it will pre-fill your request), or send an email manually to kirkcreason@creasocamps.com.

Request Account Deletion
Opens your email app with a pre-filled request.

Please include in your request:

  • The name of the app you used.
  • The email address or username associated with your account.
  • A statement confirming that you are the owner of the account.

We will verify and process your request within a reasonable time, and no later than as required by applicable law (typically within 30 days). We may need to confirm your identity before completing the deletion to protect your account.

What is deleted and what is retained

Data What happens
Account information (email, username, authentication IDs) Permanently deleted
User-generated content and app data tied to your account Permanently deleted
Analytics and diagnostic data Deleted or de-identified so it can no longer be linked to you
Transaction and payment records Retained only as required for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention obligations, then deleted

Some records may be retained for a limited period where we are legally required to keep them, or to resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. Any retained data is protected and deleted once it is no longer required.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your account or data, contact us at kirkcreason@creasocamps.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
