Privacy Policy

Creaso-Norse Technologies LLC
Effective date: July 2, 2026  •  Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Creaso-Norse Technologies LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications, web applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). It applies to all applications published by us, including those distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The specific information we collect depends on which of our applications you use and how you interact with it. Not all categories below apply to every application.

Information you provide to us

  • Account information. If an application offers accounts or sign-in, we may collect your email address, display name, and authentication identifiers (for example, when you sign in with Google).
  • Payment information. If an application offers purchases, payments are processed by our third-party payment processor (Stripe) or by the app store's billing system (Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase). We do not collect or store your full payment card number. We may receive limited transaction details such as a confirmation, the amount, and the last four digits of the card.
  • Communications. If you contact us for support, we collect the information you choose to provide, such as your email address and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage information. We and our service providers may collect device type, operating system version, app version, language settings, general usage events, and crash/diagnostic data to operate and improve the Services.
  • Advertising identifiers. Applications that display ads may access your device's advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) for the purposes described in the Advertising section below.
  • Approximate location. Some information (such as IP address) may be used to infer approximate, non-precise location for advertising and analytics. We do not collect precise GPS location unless an application explicitly requests it for a stated feature and you grant permission.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and operate the Services and their features.
  • To process purchases and deliver purchased content or functionality.
  • To create and manage accounts and authenticate users.
  • To display advertising, including personalized advertising where permitted.
  • To analyze usage, diagnose crashes, and improve performance and reliability.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate with you about the Services.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security, or technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Advertising

Some of our applications display advertisements served through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may use your device's advertising identifier and related information to serve and measure ads, including personalized (interest-based) ads where permitted by you and applicable law.

You can control ad personalization at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads, where you can reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization.
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use its services, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party providers to operate our Services. These providers process information on our behalf or as independent controllers under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase — analytics, crash reporting, authentication, and cloud data storage. Privacy & Security
  • Stripe — payment processing (for applications that take payments). Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase — in-app purchase processing, governed by the respective platform's privacy policy.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who perform functions on our behalf (such as those listed above), under agreements that limit their use of the information.
  • For legal reasons, if we believe disclosure is required to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, the public, or us.
  • In a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

Account and data deletion. If an application offers an account, you may request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Where an application provides an in-app "Delete Account" option, you can use it directly. You may also request deletion at any time by contacting us at the email address below, and we will process your request in accordance with applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

Some of our applications are intended for general audiences and are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a manner that violates applicable law such as the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Applications that are designed for or directed to children are configured to limit data collection and to serve only non-personalized advertising in accordance with platform family policies. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Security
